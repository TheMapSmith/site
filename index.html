Freelance Cartographer

Hello! My name is Stephen, and I'm passionate about maps.

I specialize in web and digital cartography, as well as data and product design.

I've designed maps for apps, for art, for clothing, and for fun.

I am well versed in Esri and Mapbox tools, as well as many open source utilities. I love OpenStreetMap. I'm enthusiastic about UI/UX, typography, design, and Oxford Commas.

Feel free to reach out for any reason. I'm happy to help!
