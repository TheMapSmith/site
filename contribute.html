Thanks for helping me out

There are over 35,000 golf courses in the world, and it would take me far too long to draw them all myself! That's why I need your help. You can help me (and the OpenStreetMap community) by adding your favorite course to the map yourself! Local knowledge is really what makes OpenStreetMap the best map in the world. You'll probably do a much better job drawing the course than I will from just aerial photos alone.

Tags

The maps I've crafted with OpenStreetMap data relies on the attributes in the data called Tags. Tags allow me to pinpoint the various shapes that make up the golf course. Below is a list of the tags that I use on the golf course:

Tag = "Value" Result
golf green Putting Green
golf fairway Fairway
golf bunker Sand Trap
golf tee Tee Box
natural tree Tree
highway path Cart Path*
foot yes Cart Path*
bridge yes Cart Path bridge*
natural wood Wooded areas
natural water Water body (pond, lake)
natural wetland Waterlogged area
natural stream Creek, stream, or brook
golf fairway Fairway

*Notice: Cart paths require multiple tags.

Get stuck?

The OpenStreetMap wiki, as well as their TagInfo site can help you if you're not sure how to tag a feature. And you can always email me stephen at mapsmith dot net.

Benefits for helping
