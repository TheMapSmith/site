Anatomy of a Map Review

Written Feedback

Thoughtful, actionable feedback. An honest, incisive assessment of your map's strengths and weaknesses. Clear solutions to improve message clarity. A summary of key ways to polish the visual design.

Included with all packages

Video Feedback

A narrated screen capture of the review, with additional feedback and comments.

Video Chat

One-on-one video conference where you get feedback in real-time. Respond to questions and give additional information to help focus the map review. A recording of the session will be provided for your reference later.

Map Annotation

Direct mark-ups on the map to provide focused feedback and areas that can be improved.
